## 🎯 Campaign Phases (UK-Focused)

### Phase 1: Awareness (Week 1-2)
- **Budget Split**: 50% video, 50% static
- **Target Audience**: 18-35, interested in shopping/fashion/tech
- **Key Message**: "Pay in 4" benefit
- **Best Variations**: A & C

### Phase 2: Conversion (Week 3-4)
- **Target**: Engaged users from Phase 1
- **Key Message**: Social proof + FOMO
- **Best Variation**: B (FOMO)
- **CTA Focus**: Clear sign-up/demo requests

### Phase 3: Retargeting (Week 5+)
- **Target**: Website visitors who didn't convert
- **Key Message**: Urgency + direct value
- **Best Variations**: D (Premium pricing) or A (Urgency)
- **Incentive**: "First purchase £5 off" (if applicable)
