## 📱 Snapchat Ad Specs

### Story Ads (Primary Format)
- **Duration**: 3-10 seconds (optimal: 5-6s)
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920 px (vertical, 9:16)
- **File Size**: Max 200MB
- **Format**: MP4 or MOV
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps

### Snap Ads (Video)
- **Duration**: 3-30 seconds
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (full-screen vertical)
- **Call-to-Action**: Swipe up to demo/sign up

### Static Image Ads
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920 px
- **Format**: PNG, JPG, GIF
- **File Size**: Max 5MB
- **Text on Image**: Max 20% of ad space

## 🎬 Video Script Ideas

## 📊 Campaign Strategy

### Phase 1: Awareness (Week 1-2)
- 50% budget on video ads (Scripts A & B)
- 50% budget on static image ads
- Target: Broad audience (18-35, interested in shopping/fashion)

### Phase 2: Conversion (Week 3-4)
- Shift to audience of engaged users from Phase 1
- Emphasize social proof copy (Variation B)
- Focus on CTA clarity

### Phase 3: Retargeting (Week 5+)
- Target website visitors who didn't convert
- Use urgency-based copy (Variation A)
- Offer incentive (if applicable): "First order $5 off"

## 💡 Creative Best Practices for Snapchat

1. **Vertical First**: Design everything for vertical viewing
2. **Hook Early**: First 2 seconds must grab attention
3. **No Sound**: Add captions/text for all dialogue
4. **Mobile Optimized**: Large, readable text
5. **Fast-Moving**: Cuts every 1-2 seconds
6. **Authentic Feel**: Avoid overly polished corporate vibe
7. **Clear CTA**: Always visible "Swipe Up" prompt
8. **Brand Consistency**: Klarna blue in every asset

## 📈 Success Metrics

- **Click-through Rate (CTR)**: Target 5-8%
- **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)**: Target $8-15 for sign-ups
- **Video View Rate**: Target 50%+ completion rate
